NCDP Coastal UAV Surveys

SpatioTemporal Asset Catalog (STAC) — Deakin University

This catalog contains UAV coastal survey data collected as part of the National Coastal Data Program (NCDP), including orthomosaics, digital surface models (DSMs), and point clouds from repeated surveys along the Victorian coastline.
